Title: Inventory Transfer Feasibility Note
Date: 2026-03-04
Author: Supply Chain Planning

North California dealers are operating with demand above available supply for premium family SUV inquiries.
Up to 150 units of CX-90 PHEV could be transferred from Southeast dealers within two weeks.
Estimated logistics cost is approximately USD 420 per unit.
Transfer is operationally feasible, but should be combined with local dealer communication to avoid channel friction.
