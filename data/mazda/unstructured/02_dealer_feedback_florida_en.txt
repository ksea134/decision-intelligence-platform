Title: Florida Dealer Council Feedback
Date: 2026-03-02
Author: Florida Dealer Council

Dealers in South Florida report that CX-90 PHEV showroom traffic is stable, yet close rates have softened.
Common customer comments:
- "I like the vehicle, but the payment is too high compared with Toyota."
- "If Mazda gave me straight cashback, I would decide today."
- "I want premium value, not a discount-brand experience."

Dealer recommendation:
A financing-led response would be easier to position than blunt price cuts because sales staff can defend monthly affordability while preserving the image of the vehicle.
