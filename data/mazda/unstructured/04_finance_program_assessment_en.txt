Title: MFS Program Assessment for CX-90 PHEV
Date: 2026-03-03
Author: Mazda Financial Services

Findings:
- In Southeast markets, a 0.9% APR / 72-month offer improves approval and conversion materially.
- Payment reduction is approximately USD 85 to USD 95 per month versus the current standard APR.
- Default risk remains manageable and does not increase materially under the promotional APR scenario.

Recommendation:
Use financing support first. Cashback should be reserved only for aging inventory that remains unsold after targeted financing and dealer transfer actions.
