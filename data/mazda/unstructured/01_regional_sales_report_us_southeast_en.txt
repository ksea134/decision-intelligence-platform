Title: Southeast US Regional Sales Weekly Report
Date: 2026-03-01
Author: MNAO Southeast Regional Office

Summary:
CX-90 PHEV inventory has begun to accumulate in Florida and Georgia. Dealers report that affluent households remain interested in the vehicle, but conversion is slowing because monthly payments now exceed the psychological threshold of about USD 850 in many financing scenarios.

Detail:
1. Miami and Atlanta dealers repeatedly state that shoppers compare monthly payment, not MSRP.
2. Toyota Highlander PHEV cashback campaigns of around USD 3,000 are being cited in showroom conversations.
3. Dealers are requesting immediate price action, but some field leaders warn that repeated cash discounting could damage Mazda's premium trajectory and residual value.
4. Test-drive completion remains healthy; the biggest loss occurs between financing quote and order signature.
