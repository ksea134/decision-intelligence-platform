# FY2025 Q4 Internal Audit Summary
**Region:** North America Operations / SE Zone
**Scope:** Labor Compliance & Operational Efficiency

## Executive Summary
The post-merger integration (PMI) of former Speedway locations continues to progress according to the strategic roadmap. 
A key achievement in the SE Zone is the 15% year-over-year reduction in reported overtime expenses, reflecting the successful implementation of the new labor scheduling system. While a limited number of internal grievances have been filed via EthicsPoint, they have been classified as "Individual Personnel Matters" and are being handled by local HR. No material legal risks to the enterprise have been identified at this time.
